# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 13, 2026**

Quran AI - Learn & Recite ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through the App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The App is designed to be used without requiring a user account or the collection of personally identifiable information. 
- **Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or physical location.
- **Audio Data**: The "Recitation" feature requires access to your device's microphone. Audio data is processed in real-time for transcription and scoring. We do not store your voice recordings on our servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App may use third-party services (such as Vercel or OpenAI/Groq) to provide AI-powered features (Tafseer, Chat, and Insights). These services receive only the text data required to generate responses and do not receive any personal identification of the user.

## 3. Data Storage
Any bookmarks, progress, or settings are stored locally on your device. Clearing the App data or uninstalling the App will remove this information.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App is intended for use by a general audience, including children under the guidance of parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@islamicai.quran.
